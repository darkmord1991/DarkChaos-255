Key Findings from "Noa-1995" & Similar Projects:
------------------------------------------------
- Texture Atlases: Essential for performance and managing the thousands of small retail UI assets.
- Virtual Frames: Used extensively to replicate the "template" system of Retail XML.
- Lighting: `model:SetLight` is crucial for the "modern" look of character models.
- Font: Custom fonts (Friz Quadrata with specific spacing) make a huge difference.
