## World Events

### 1. Naga Invasion (Every 4 Hours)
**Trigger:** Server time-based
**Duration:** 30 minutes
**Mechanics:**
- Naga waves spawn at 3 locations (Valormok, Ton'ma, Northern Checkpoint)
- Players defend NPCs from waves
- Final wave includes elite Naga Warlord
- Contribution tracking for rewards

**Rewards:**
| Contribution | Reward |
|--------------|--------|
| 10 kills | Bronze Token |
| 25 kills | Silver Token |
| 50 kills | Gold Token |
| Boss kill | Participation chest |

**Tokens Exchange:** Cosmetics, XP boosts, mounts

---

### 3. Dragon Flight Visit (Weekly)
**Trigger:** Sunday 8 PM server time
**Duration:** 2 hours
**Location:** Dragon Coast

**Mechanics:**

- Special quests available only during event

**Unique Rewards:**

## Special Mechanics

### 1. Arcane Corruption Stacks
**Zones Affected:** 5-8 (Demon/Temple areas)
**Mechanic:**
- Standing in corrupted areas adds stacks
- 0-10 stacks: Visual effect only
- 11-25 stacks: -5% healing received
- 26-50 stacks: -10% damage dealt
- 50+ stacks: Teleport to nearest base

**Cleansing:**
- Return to faction base
- Use cleansing items (craftable)
- Find cleansing shrines

---

### 2. Ancient Power System
**Location:** Temple of Eternity only
**Mechanic:**
- Collect "Echo Fragments" from Temple mobs
- Use at Ancient Altars for temporary buffs
- Buffs last 30 minutes, don't persist through death

| Fragments | Buff |
|-----------|------|
| 5 | +5% damage |
| 10 | +10% damage, +5% HP |
| 25 | +15% damage, +10% HP, glow effect |
| 50 | All above + unique spell unlock |

---

### 3. Ghostly Vision Mode
**Trigger:** Use item "Spectral Lens"
**Duration:** 10 minutes
**Effect:**
- See hidden ghost NPCs
- Interact with ghostly objects
- Access hidden quest givers
- Discover secret areas

**Source:** Quest reward, rare drop, crafted

## Boss Mechanics Ideas

### Varo'then's Ghost (D1 Boss)
**Abilities:**
1. **Shadow Bolt Volley** - AoE damage
2. **Ghostly Charge** - Charges random player
3. **Phase Shift** - Disappears, spawns adds
4. **Queen's Wrath** (20%) - Enrage phase

---

### Duke Hydraxis (D3 Boss)
**Abilities:**
1. **Water Bolt** - Primary attack
2. **Tidal Wave** - Knockback in cone
3. **Summon Elemental** - Adds every 25%
4. **Deep Freeze** - Tank debuff (requires swap)

---

### Azuregos (D5 Boss)
**Abilities:**
1. **Arcane Barrage** - Heavy magic damage
2. **Frost Breath** - Cone freeze
3. **Teleport** - Random location
4. **Mana Storm** - Drains & damages mana users
5. **Reflection** - Spell reflect shield
