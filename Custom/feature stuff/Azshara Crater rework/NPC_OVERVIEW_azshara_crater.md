## Leveling Quest Ideas

### Zone 1: Valormok Rim (Level 1-10)
**Theme:** Learning the land, wildlife threats

| Quest Name | Level | Type | Objective | NPC |
|------------|-------|------|-----------|-----|
| **Welcome to the Crater** | 1 | Intro | Speak with Commander Bloodfist | Bloodfist |
| **Thinning the Pack** | 3 | Kill | Slay 8 Crater Wolves | Guard NPC |
| **Bear Necessities** | 5 | Collect | Gather 6 Bear Pelts | Leatherworker |
| **Strange Wisps** | 7 | Kill | Destroy 5 Highborne Wisps | Shaman |
| **The Northern Road** | 10 | Travel | Report to Northern Checkpoint | Commander |

**Chain Quest: "Whispers from the Past"**
1. (Lv 5) Find ancient night elf relics in the rim
2. (Lv 8) Bring them to the shaman for analysis
3. (Lv 10) Learn about the crater's dark history → leads to Zone 2
